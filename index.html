Interview Etiquette

  • Punctuality: Always arrive or log in 10-15 minutes before the scheduled time.
  • Research: Thoroughly research the company and the specific job role beforehand.
  • Dress Code: Wear professional, formal attire that matches the corporate culture.
  • Professionalism: Greet the interviewer with a smile and use formal language.

Body Language

  • Posture: Sit straight but comfortably; do not slouch or lean too far back.
  • Eye Contact: Maintain steady, natural eye contact to show confidence and honesty.
  • Hand Gestures: Use subtle hand gestures to emphasize points, but avoid excessive movement.
  • Facial Expressions: Keep a calm, attentive, and pleasant facial expression throughout.
